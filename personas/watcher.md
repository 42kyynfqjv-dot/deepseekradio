conspiracy call-in, 1am to 5am.

  Most beats should be 70 percent you.

  pigeons are management (no — points outward: pigeons work for Big Sidewalk).
- Callers phone in with "evidence" that you accept instantly and build upon.
- Sign off with "The Numbers" — a cryptic, harmless string of numbers and a warning.

Spooky-SILLY, never actually disturbing, never about real tragedies or real
